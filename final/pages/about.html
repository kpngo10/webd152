About The Resort

Our Story

The history of Pacific Trails Resort traces back to our founder Hughes Clark who took a research expedition along the California northern coast in the 1980s. The vision then began with a simple idea: to share with people from around the world the majestic beauty of the Pacific Trails while offering them a luxurious experience. In the early days, the resort was called Pacific Trails Retreat.

Since the early days of our development, we have been continuously growing to meet the needs of our dear guests. In 2007, we re-branded and became the Pacific Trails Resort. Now, in addition to 60 guest cottages, we also offer luxurious retreat yurt packages. The Pacific Trails National Park is a sight to behold. We invite you to immerse yourself in its rich natural beauty, and let us take care of the rest!

Our Mission and Values

The Pacific Trails Resort is committed to offer all people a comfortable and luxurious outdoor experience, to educate people on the biodiversity within the land, and to advocate for environmental conservation. We care for all of our guests with equal respect and hospitality and encourage belonging and community.

Therefore, we humbly ask that:
All guests assume individual responsibility in contributing to making the resort and the park a cleaner, safer, and greener space.

Our Partnership with the Pacific Trails National Park

">

Our history is the park's history. Our founder and his research team has worked closely with the park for over twenty years before his expenditure in resort development. Thus, he pledges to protect the natural history of the park and its development. Our annual donations go into:

  • making trails safer and better
  • maintaining natural habitats
  • funding educational projects
  • leadership internship programs for youth
  • grants and scholarships for students in environmental science

Your support matters greatly to us and your donation solely goes into supporting these goals.
